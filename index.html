Trusted Cleaning Professionals Across Regional Australia

PlanetShine is a locally operated cleaning company proudly based in the Hunter Region of New South Wales. Backed by more than 5 years of industry experience, we provide reliable residential, commercial and specialised cleaning services for homes, offices, retail premises, schools, hospitality venues, strata properties and industrial facilities.

Our commitment is simple — deliver professional cleaning services with exceptional attention to detail, transparent communication and outstanding customer service. Whether you require a one-off deep clean, regular maintenance cleaning, end-of-lease cleaning or a large commercial cleaning contract, our team works to the highest standards to ensure every property is presented at its best.

From Scone, Aberdeen, Muswellbrook, Singleton, Maitland and Newcastle through to regional communities across Australia, PlanetShine continues to build trusted relationships with homeowners, businesses and property managers seeking dependable cleaning solutions.
