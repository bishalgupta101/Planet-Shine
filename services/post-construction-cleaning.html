Post-Construction Cleaning across regional Australia

Construction sites leave behind dust, debris, paint splatter and adhesive residue that require specialist cleaning before handover. PlanetShine's post-construction team ensures your residential or commercial build is spotless and ready for occupation or inspection.

What's included

  • Dust and debris removal from all surfaces
  • Window cleaning with paint and adhesive removal
  • Floor cleaning and polishing
  • Bathroom and kitchen detail cleaning
  • Removal of stickers, labels and protective film
  • Cabinet and wardrobe interior cleaning
  • Final vacuum and mop
  • External area and entry cleaning
