About PlanetShine

An Australian-owned cleaning company built on quality — now serving the entire country, from Sydney CBD to the most remote corners of regional Australia.

Our story

Started regional. Built for all of Australia.

PlanetShine was founded with a clear purpose — to deliver genuinely premium cleaning services that Australians can rely on, regardless of where they live or work. What started in regional NSW has grown into a nationwide provider without ever compromising the quality and personal attention that defines everything we do.

We are currently active across Sydney Metro, the Hunter Region — including Newcastle, Maitland, Singleton, Muswellbrook and Scone — New England and Northern Tablelands including Tamworth and Armidale, and Central West NSW including Bathurst, Orange and Dubbo. Our network is expanding rapidly across every Australian state and territory.

We don't believe quality cleaning should be a luxury available only to certain postcodes. Whether you're in a Sydney high-rise or a regional farmhouse, PlanetShine delivers the same exceptional standard every time.
