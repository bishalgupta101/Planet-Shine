About PlanetShine

A cleaning company built for the regions — focused on the communities that deserve premium service.

Our story

Built for regional Australia

PlanetShine was founded with a clear focus — to deliver professional, premium cleaning to the regional towns and communities across Australia that are too often left behind by city-based providers.

We are currently active throughout the Hunter Region in NSW, and rapidly growing our network across Queensland, the Northern Territory, Western Australia, South Australia and regional Victoria. Our focus is and always will be on regional Australia — not the crowded, competitive city markets.

This focus means we can offer faster response times, more personalised service and a genuine connection to the communities where we work.
